The Legend of Rani Padmini

The wind carried whispers of ash and sorrow across the ravaged plains, painting the dawn a grim, charcoal grey. Skeletal structures of once-proud palaces stood against the bruised sky, silent witnesses to a tragedy etched in blood and fire. This was Chittor, a kingdom reduced to ruins, its story a tapestry woven from threads of defiance, obsession, and a truth obscured by the smoke of a thousand burning pyres. This is not the beginning. This is the end. Or perhaps, only one ending.
